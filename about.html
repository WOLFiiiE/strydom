About Us

For 15 years, Strydom Stop & Stay has been the epitome of comfort and quality stays in Klerksdorp

For an impressive 15 years, Strydom Stop & Stay has been the embodiment of warm South African hospitality, welcoming countless travelers and long-term guests to Klerksdorp. Nestled in the heart of our vibrant city, our guesthouse is more than just a place to rest your head; it's a testament to the enduring values of comfort and genuine hospitality.

We take pride in creating memorable experiences for everyone, whether you're here for business, leisure, or to explore the cultural richness of Klerksdorp. With 15 years of expertise, our commitment to seamless, satisfying stays continues to stand strong. Join us and become a part of our enduring story, where each guest contributes to our rich tapestry of South African charm and warmth.

Experience the Art of Hospitality with Us

Security

We have a secure and monitored entrance with controlled access as well as lockable rooms. Surveillance cameras are strategically placed around the property.

Open Kitchen

The open kitchen allows guests the freedom to cook and dine at their own pace in a clean and well-maintained facility.

WIFI Areas

Wi-Fi is available throughout the guesthouse, ensuring that guests can stay connected in common areas, rooms and outdoor spaces.

Parking Area

The parking area is spacious, providing ample room for guests to park their vehicles comfortably with firm and great security.
